Privacy Policy

Home | Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 25th March, 2025

PayContinental respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information: Name, email address, and contact details provided during account registration.

Transaction Data: Records of crypto transactions conducted through PayContinental.

Technical Data: Device information, IP addresses, and browsing behavior on our platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Enhance security and fraud prevention measures
  • Send important updates and promotional materials (with user consent)

3. Data Protection Measures

We implement industry-standard encryption for sensitive data, secure authentication protocols to protect user accounts, and conduct regular security audits to prevent data breaches.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell or share user data for marketing purposes. We may disclose information to comply with legal obligations or security investigations.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to enhance user experience and analyze site traffic. Users can manage cookie preferences through browser settings.

6. User Rights & Control

Users can request data access, correction, or deletion. Opt-out options for marketing communications are available.

7. Retention Policy

We retain user data only as long as necessary for service provision and legal compliance.

8. Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time.

9. Contact Us

For privacy concerns, email us at support@paycontinental.com.
